1.  "Maxim" – The Strategic Leader (Owner/CEO): Needs to link finance with strategy, fears losing control, wants to increase business value.
2.  "Natalia" – The Finance Transformer (CFO/Financial Director): Wants to transform the finance function, gain influence, and become a strategic partner.
3.  "Sergei" – The Career Transitioner (Professional from another field): Aims to master corporate finance, change careers without losing experience.

- Headline: "Your Strategic Partner in Finance. From Operational Numbers to Strategic Growth."

**Products/Services (Structure the page around these):**
1.  **Consulting (Project Work):** Due Diligence, financial function optimization, M&A strategy, crisis management.

3.  **Retainer (Independent Advisory):** Monthly strategic partnership, participation in board meetings, regular financial health audits.

1.  **Hero Section:** Strong headline, sub-headline, high-quality professional portrait photo, clear Call-to-Action (e.g., "Schedule a Strategy Session").
2.  **For Whom Section:** Visually distinct blocks for the three personas ("Maxim", "Natalia", "Sergei") with their key problem and the offered solution.
3.  **Services/Products Section:** Detailed, benefit-oriented description of the three product lines (Consulting, Mentoring, Retainer).

6.  **Contact & Call-to-Action:** A clear contact form linked to a CRM/email, phone number, and link to Telegram/WhatsApp.

- **Color Palette:** Professional blues (trust, stability), accent greens (growth), possibly deep red for important CTAs. Avoid being too corporate or cold.

**Functionality:**

- A functional contact form placeholder.
